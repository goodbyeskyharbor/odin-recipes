Adult Hot Cocoa

Description

Let's be real: if you're of legal drinking age, you might have realized that plain, old hot cocoa isn't doing the trick anymore. Luckily, I have the perfect solution for you and it's better than eggnog cocktails.

This holiday, or really any time of the year, spice up the party with a hot chocolate-vodka mix tailored perfectly to your liking. Keep reading to learn about this awesome recipe.

Ingredients

  • Kahlua Coffee Liqueur (rum-based, coffee flavored liqueur)
  • Bailey's Irish Cream (whiskey-based, cream liqueur with mild chocolate flavor)
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (preferably Hershey's brand)
  • Salt
  • Milk (the more fat, the creamier)

Steps

  1. Add sugar, cocoa powder, and salt to an airtight container, and stir or shake (with the lid ON) to combine.
  2. Heat between 3/4 cup and 1 cup of milk until hot.
  3. For every cup of hot milk, add 2 generous tablespoons of the hot cocoa mix.
  4. Stir until dissolved.
  5. For a single serving, add 1 ounce of Kahlua and 1 ounce of Baileys per serving of hot chocolate.
  6. Stir for a few seconds and serve.
  7. Optional: Top it off with miniature marshmallows or whipped cream.
