Thai Iced Coffee

Description

If you're looking for a sweet, creamy, and strong coffee drink, look no further. This Thai iced coffee recipe blends your favorite brand of coffee bean in with a rich texture to achieve the sole purpose of maximum deliciousness.

Visit Thailand and you'll quickly discover this iced coffee drink is the perfect remedy to a hot day on the streets or at the beach. Luckily, if you don't have the means of visiting Thailand today, you can still experience the creamy goodness of this drink right from the comfort of your own home. Continue reading and I'll show you how to fend off even the hottest of days!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (16 ounces) of strongly brewed hot coffee (or a strong instant coffee)
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk (or a sugar-free alternative)
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk (or fresh heavy cream)

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Pour your hot coffee into a container for mixing/pouring.
  3. Add your choice of condensed milk to taste and stir well until it dissolves.
  4. Add a few ice cubes and refrigerate for no less than an hour.
  5. Serve by placing a couple ice cubes in the bottom of your glass and pour the coffee in.
  6. Top off your drink with 2 to 3 tablespoons of evaporated milk or heavy cream.
